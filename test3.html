Colombo To Kandy

The journey from Colombo to Kandy offers scenic landscapes and lush greenery, connecting the commercial capital with the cultural heart of Sri Lanka. Kandy is renowned for its rich history and the Temple of the Sacred Tooth Relic.

Colombo To Kandy

Traveling from Colombo to Galle along the southwestern coast provides stunning ocean views and stops at beautiful beaches. Galle is famous for its well-preserved Dutch colonial architecture and historic fort.
